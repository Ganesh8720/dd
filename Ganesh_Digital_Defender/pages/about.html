About Project

Social media is the fastest-growing technology in the world. Some common issues on social media include harassment, spreading rumors, sending malicious messages or posting inappropriate content, and sharing someone's private information. Traditional methods of detecting these issues are often ineffective due to the dynamic nature of online interaction. The primary objective of this project is to identify cyberbullies on social media platforms using various deep-learning techniques We gather data from various social media platforms, including text messages and images. This data undergoes preprocessing using NLP (natural language processing) techniques such as Bag of Words (BoW) for text classification and sentiment analysis to identify harmful content. To enhance detection, we employ advanced methods like BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers), and CNN (Convolutional Neural Networks) for more precise and context-aware detection. The deep learning models help exhibit higher accuracy and effectiveness in detecting cyberbullies on various social media platforms.
